Our Roots

Sprout & Grow began as a small neighborhood project for friends and families who wanted to grow healthier, greener spaces at home. Today, we’re a community nursery focused on organic plants, sustainable gardening practices, and friendly, hands-on support.

We partner with local growers, stock eco-conscious soil and supplies, and host workshops that make gardening less intimidating and more fun. Whether you’re brand new to houseplants or planning your next vegetable garden, we’re here to help you grow with confidence.
