Events & Workshops

Learn, dig in, and grow with us. Join our small-group workshops and family-friendly events throughout the season.

Organic Gardening Basics – Weekend Class

A relaxed, beginner-friendly workshop covering soil health, plant selection, watering, and natural pest control. Great for new gardeners who want to start an organic balcony, backyard, or raised-bed garden.

Saturdays · 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM · At the nursery

Compost 101: Turning Waste into Soil Gold

Learn how to turn kitchen scraps and yard waste into rich, healthy compost. We’ll cover composting methods, what to add (and what to avoid), and how to use finished compost to feed your garden.

Select Thursdays · 6:00 PM – 7:30 PM · At the nursery

Kids’ Garden Day – Family Workshop

A hands-on day for kids to plant, explore, and learn where their food comes from. We’ll decorate pots, plant easy-to-grow seeds, and talk about how to care for them at home.

One Sunday each month · 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM · Ages 5–12 with guardian
